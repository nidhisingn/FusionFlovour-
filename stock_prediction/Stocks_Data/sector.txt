======================================================================
                     SECTOR-WISE IDEAL RANGES (Quick Guide)
======================================================================

How to use:
  • Compare stocks only within the same sector
  • Treat these as guidelines, not strict rules
  • Always check trends over 3–5 years (not a single year)


----------------------------------------------------------------------
IT / SOFTWARE
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Parameter            Ideal Range / Notes
------------------  --------------------------------------------------
Revenue Growth       10–20% YoY
EBITDA Margin        22–35%
Net Profit Margin    15–25%
ROE                  25–40%
D/E                  0–0.2 (almost zero debt)
FCF                  Very strong, close to net profit
P/E                  25–35x
P/B                  5–10x

Notes:
  IT companies are asset-light, high-margin, low-debt businesses.
  Very high ROE and strong FCF are expected.


----------------------------------------------------------------------
FMCG (HUL, Nestle, Asian Paints, Britannia)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Parameter            Ideal Range / Notes
------------------  --------------------------------------------------
Revenue Growth       8–15% YoY
EBITDA Margin        18–28%
Net Profit Margin    12–20%
ROE                  30–80% (often very high due to low equity base)
D/E                  0–0.3
FCF                  Strong and consistent
P/E                  50–80x (premium for quality)
P/B                  10–30x

Notes:
  FMCG commands premium valuations due to brand moat + stable cash flows.
  ROE can look extremely high because they need little capital to operate.


----------------------------------------------------------------------
BANKING & NBFC (HDFC Bank, ICICI, Kotak, Bajaj Finance)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Parameter            Ideal Range / Notes
------------------  --------------------------------------------------
Revenue Growth (NII) 15–25% YoY
NIM                  3–5% (replaces EBITDA margin)
Net Profit Margin    20–30%
ROE                  15–20%
D/E                  Not applicable (banks have structurally high leverage)
GNPA                 Below 2% (key risk metric)
P/E                  15–25x
P/B                  2–4x

Notes:
  For banks, D/E is not useful. Focus on NIM + NPA (asset quality).
  GNPA above ~5% is usually a serious warning sign.


----------------------------------------------------------------------
PHARMA (Sun Pharma, Dr Reddy's, Cipla, Divi's)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Parameter            Ideal Range / Notes
------------------  --------------------------------------------------
Revenue Growth       10–18% YoY
EBITDA Margin        20–35%
Net Profit Margin    12–20%
ROE                  15–25%
D/E                  0–0.5
FCF                  Moderate (R&D spending is high)
P/E                  25–40x
P/B                  3–6x

Notes:
  Watch R&D as % of revenue (higher can mean better pipeline).
  US FDA approvals/rejections can directly impact price.


----------------------------------------------------------------------
INFRASTRUCTURE / REAL ESTATE (L&T, DLF, Adani Ports)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Parameter            Ideal Range / Notes
------------------  --------------------------------------------------
Revenue Growth       12–20% YoY
EBITDA Margin        15–22%
Net Profit Margin    8–15%
ROE                  12–18%
D/E                  1–3 (high debt is normal)
FCF                  Often negative (heavy capex phase)
P/E                  20–35x
P/B                  2–4x

Notes:
  Negative FCF can be acceptable due to heavy capex.
  Judge by order book size, execution quality, and cash conversion.


----------------------------------------------------------------------
AUTO (Maruti, Tata Motors, M&M, Bajaj Auto)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Parameter            Ideal Range / Notes
------------------  --------------------------------------------------
Revenue Growth       8–15% YoY
EBITDA Margin        12–18%
Net Profit Margin    6–12%
ROE                  15–25%
D/E                  0–0.5

Notes:

----------------------------------------------------------------------
STEEL / METALS (Tata Steel, JSW, Hindalco)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Parameter            Ideal Range / Notes
------------------  --------------------------------------------------
Revenue Growth       Cyclical — varies widely
EBITDA Margin        15–25% (depends on commodity cycle)
Net Profit Margin    6–12%
ROE                  10–20%
D/E                  0.5–2
FCF                  Cyclical — strong in upcycle
P/E                  6–12x (often low because cyclical)
P/B                  1–2x

Notes:
  Cyclical business: profits swing with global commodity prices.
  Buy when P/E looks HIGH (bad cycle, low profits),
  sell when P/E looks LOW (good cycle, peak profits).
  This is opposite of normal valuation logic.
